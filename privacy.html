Privacy Policy

At Kartik Painter Services, your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit or interact with our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

When you contact or interact with Kartik Painter Services, we may collect:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number (when you fill out our contact form).
  • Any details you voluntarily provide about painting services or inquiries.
  • Technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and device information.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for:

  • Responding to your queries and service requests.
  • Providing you with accurate painting service information and updates.
  • Improving our website performance and customer experience.
  • Sending occasional promotional offers related to Kartik Painter Services.
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal details with any third party, except when required by law.

3. Cookies Policy

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some parts of the website may not function properly.

4. Data Protection & Security

We take appropriate measures to safeguard your personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. All information shared with Kartik Painter Services is kept secure and confidential.

5. Third-Party Services

Sometimes, we may use trusted third-party tools (such as Google Maps or review widgets) to improve your experience. These third parties have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Ask for corrections if your data is inaccurate.
  • Request deletion of your data from our records (where legally possible).
